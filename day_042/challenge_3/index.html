My top 5 Favourite Websites

  1. Wikipedia - A vast resource of information on virtually any topic.
  2. Reddit - A community-driven platform for discussions and sharing content.
  3. GitHub - A platform for version control and collaboration on coding projects.
  4. Stack Overflow - A Q&A site for programmers to find solutions to coding problems.
  5. Medium - A platform for reading and writing articles on a variety of topics.
